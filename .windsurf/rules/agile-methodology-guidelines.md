# Agile Best Practices

## Story Breakdown
- Max 5 story points per story (1 point = 8 hours)
- Independent and deliverable as standalone units
- Testable independently with business value
- Follow INVEST principles (Independent, Negotiable, Valuable, Estimable, Small, Testable)

## Task Creation
- Max task effort: 8 hours
- Manageable and meaningful
- - Distinct tasks for UI/Frontend, Server/Backend/API, Database/Schema/Migrations, DevOps/CI/CD/Infrastructure, and QA/Testing as needed
- Clear acceptance criteria
